How the Engine Works

Four interconnected systems running in parallel as you play.

[ 01 ]

Decision Engine

Every choice cascades. A bad call early compounds into worse options later. Decisions are non-reversible — just like real disasters.

[ 02 ]

Live Stat Tracking

Health, stamina, oxygen, and panic update in real time. Let any one drop to zero and the simulation ends. You get a reason why.
